Nancy Drew

Nancy Drew is the title character and the main protagonist of the Nancy Drew franchise beginning with the Nancy Drew Mystery Stories. Nancy is a teenage, amateur detective who lives with her father, Carson Drew, in the small town of River Heights. She is often assisted on her crime investigations by her two best friends, Bess Marvin and George Fayne, and occasionally by the Hardy Boys or her boyfriend Ned Nickerson.


Biography

Nancy Drew's mother died when Nancy was only three years old. The Drew's housekeeper, Hannah Gruen, has since become like a mother to Nancy. Nancy has a very good relationship with her father, whose lawyering job tends to call him away on business quite often. Nancy has a dog called Togo. She lives in a town called River Heights.


Appearance

Nancy is around 16-18 years old and is commonly described as having red-blond hair and blue eyes. She is often said to have a "timeless" look when it comes to her clothing choices, wearing them more for comfort than for fashion styles.
